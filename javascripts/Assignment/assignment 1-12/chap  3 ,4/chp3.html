      2. Ans: A variable name can't contain any spaces.

    2. Variable names can only contain letters , numbers, dollar signs, and underscores..

      For example $my_1stVariable

    3. Variables must begin with a Capital letters , small letter, dollar signs, and underscores..

      For example $name, _name or name
